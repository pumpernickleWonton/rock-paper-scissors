How to Make: Rock, Paper, Scissors!

1. Have the Computer select a move
2. Have the Player Select a move
3. Compare the two Moves
4. Decide the Winner of the match
5. Increase the Winner's score
6. Repeat steps 1-5 until either side has 5 Points
7. At 5 points, announce the winner of the Game
8. Ask the Player if they would like to play again
9. Reset the Game
